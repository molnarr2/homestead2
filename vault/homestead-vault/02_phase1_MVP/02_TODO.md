  My recommendation: Keep Pro unlimited, no fourth tier.

  Free: 5 animals
  Standard ($4.99/mo): 25 animals
  Pro ($9.99/mo): Unlimited animals

  Why not cap Pro and add a fourth:

  - Your target users are homesteaders, not enterprise ranchers. Someone with 200+ head is using cattle-specific software, not a general homestead app.
  - The jump from $9.99 to a $19.99+ "Ranch" tier would serve a tiny slice of your users — not worth the added gating logic and support overhead.
  - Unlimited at Pro feels generous and creates goodwill. Users hate discovering yet another ceiling after already paying.

  If you really want more revenue from heavy users, a better lever is feature-gating on Pro rather than a fourth tier:

  - Multi-homestead support (Pro only)
  - Export/reports (Pro only)
  - Photo storage beyond X per animal (Pro only)

  This way your pricing stays simple (3 tiers, animal count is the main gate) but Pro feels premium through features, not just a higher animal cap.
