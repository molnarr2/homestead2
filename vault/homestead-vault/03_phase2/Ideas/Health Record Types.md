# Health Record Types

## Current (MVP)
- **Vaccination** — routine shots, boosters
- **Medication** — antibiotics, pain relief, ongoing treatments
- **Deworming** — internal/external parasite treatment
- **Vet Visit** — checkups, diagnostics, procedures
- **Illness** — sickness tracking with symptoms/resolution
- **Injury** — wound/trauma tracking with outcome

## Future Candidates
- **Surgery/Procedure** — anesthesia, recovery, suture removal (spays, C-sections, castration, dehorning)
- **Hoof/Foot Trim** — goats, horses, cattle; has its own cadence
- **Dental** — horses (teeth floating), goats/sheep
- **Testing/Lab Work** — fecal egg counts, blood panels, disease testing (CL, CAE, Coggins)
- **Quarantine** — isolation periods for new arrivals or sick animals

## Animal-Specific Notes
- **Poultry**: health is usually flock-level, not individual
- **Horses**: dental and hoof care are practically their own categories
- **Cattle/Goats**: hoof trimming and parasite testing (FAMACHA scores) are routine
- **Pigs**: tusk trimming is somewhat unique
